Dr. Srinivas Manohar

BDS, MDS – Prosthodontist & Oral Implantologist

Dr. Srinivas Manohar is a distinguished dental surgeon who specializes in replacing missing teeth structures (Prosthodontics) and surgical dental implant placements. With over 11 years of active clinical practice in Andhra Pradesh, he is recognized for his precision work in full mouth restorations, fixed dental bridges, cosmetic laminates, and advanced laser-assisted micro-surgeries.
