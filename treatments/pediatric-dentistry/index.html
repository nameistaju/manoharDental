Pediatric Dentistry

Children require specialized dental care. Primary (milk) teeth act as path guides for permanent teeth to erupt correctly. Early tooth decay in children can cause severe pain, affect eating patterns, and lead to poor speech development. Pediatric dentistry focuses on preventive therapies, cavity shields, and positive dentist habit-forming.

Overview
