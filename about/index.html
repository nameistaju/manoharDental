Our History

A Legacy of Smile Excellence in Visakhapatnam

"A smile is the foundation of a brighter future." At Manohar Dental Clinic, we translate this philosophy into reality daily.

Founded by Dr. Srinivas Manohar, MDS, our clinic has established itself as one of the leading dental centers in Visakhapatnam, Santhipuram. Dr. Manohar brings over 11 years of extensive clinical practice in Prosthodontics and Implantology, focusing on complex full-mouth reconstructions, surgical dental implants, and dental aesthetics.

We pride ourselves on offering an ethical, painless, and transparent treatment environment. From root canal therapy and smile restorations to orthodontic braces, kids dentistry, and gum treatments, our specialized team uses high-grade dental materials and the latest techniques, all structured within accessible and reasonable price points.
